Welcome to Lovedale Castle

Jeevadhara foundation is a registered trust established by P J Joseph and his family.The trust is intented towards establishing a senior citizen retirement home in the name of LOVEDALE CASTLE.
LOVEDALE CASTLE is being established for a happy,contended living of senior citizens on a no profit - no loss basis.

Lovedale Castle is established for the happy,independent and healthy living for the senior citizens on a no profit - no loss basis.Hence for self-sustenance, an advance deposit and monthly charges for food and accomodation, are collected.
